Every Thursday

PHLAs Care Camp

  • We guide and take sick people to hospitals. Due to lack of literacy, many are unable to consult specialists. We also conduct medical camps in various places.
  • We conduct 11 PHLAs gatherings in six districts: Krishnagiri, Dharmapuri, Vellore, Salem, Namakkal, and Trichy. During these gatherings, we share the Gospel and provide nutritional supplements and lunch.
  • Christian counselling is provided to help them lead a spiritually strengthened and hopeful life. Patients are also counselled to take medications regularly.
  • We arrange marriages for PHLAs-positive children.
  • We conduct and participate in funeral services for PHLAs patients.
  • We maintain strict confidentiality and continuously pray for them.
Time: 9:00 AM – 2:00 PM

Service Locations

Kottaibyrahalli, Poovalmaduvu, Sellamudi

2nd Saturady & 3rd Thursday

Bible College Classes

Our Bible College classes provide in-depth theological training and leadership development for aspiring ministers and lay leaders. The program covers a wide range of topics, including Biblical studies, church history, Christian doctrine, pastoral care, and ministry skills. Through both classroom teaching and practical ministry experience, students are equipped to serve effectively in their local churches and communities.

Time: 10:30 AM – 4:30 PM
Month of December

Special Sunday School Programs

In the month of December, we conduct special Sunday School programs. During the summer holidays, we organize Vacation Bible School (VBS) and special youth retreats. Around 500 children from Kottaibyrahalli and 200 children from Poovalmaduvu participate in these programs, hear the Gospel, and receive gifts distributed by our team.

Annual Event

Medical Camp

We conduct free medical camps twice a year to provide essential healthcare services to the local community. During these camps, medical check-ups and basic treatments are offered to those in need, ensuring that even individuals with limited access to healthcare receive proper attention. In addition to healthcare support, we also share the Gospel, providing spiritual encouragement and guidance to the attendees. These camps serve as an opportunity to demonstrate Christ’s love through both practical help and spiritual outreach.

Annual Event
Month of May

Love Feast

The Love Feast is organized regularly to foster fellowship and community among believers. Every month, three main fellowship feasts are held: on the first Sunday, a feast is arranged for PLHA gatherings with 100–125 participants; on the second Saturday, a feast is held for Bible College students, leaders, and pastors with 30–40 participants; and on the third Sunday, a feast is organized for Master of Ministry course participants with 40–60 attendees. In addition to these, love feasts are conducted during special gatherings. Locations include Poovalmaduvu, where a Love Feast is held every Sunday, and Kottaibyrahalli, where it takes place every first Saturday and on special occasions. These feasts serve as opportunities for fellowship, spiritual encouragement, and sharing the love of Christ.

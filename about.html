About Us

Our Story, Our Vision, and Our Faith

Our Story

  • Dharmapuri is one of the most backward districts in Tamil Nadu and has a very small Christian population. As a family, we started our ministry in Dharmapuri by choosing a highly populated area and living in a rented home.

  • We reached out to the community by sharing the Gospel through tract distribution and Jesus film screenings, and by practicing wholistic evangelism such as guiding the sick to hospitals and conducting free medical camps.

  • We supported needy children by helping them get admission into free schools, providing educational assistance such as fees, notebooks, and school bags, and offering Christian counseling to people suffering from depression.

Our Vision

Our vision is to establish a mission field in unreached areas every 5 years and to build a church in those areas every 10 years.

Our Mission

Through wholistic evangelism, we aim to serve communities in need. Over 41 years of ministry in Dharmapuri district, Tamil Nadu, 4 churches have been planted, with the first church handed over to the Methodist Church of India.

Children's Ministry

Children are a heritage from the Lord. We run Vacation Bible Schools (VBS), Sunday Schools, and tuition centers for underprivileged children, ensuring they receive both spiritual and secular education.

  • Sunday School Programs
  • Educational Sponsorship
  • Orphan Care Support

PLHA Care

We provide comprehensive care for People Living with HIV/AIDS (PLHA). Our support includes nutritional supplements, medical counseling, and emotional support groups to combat stigma and ensure a dignified life.

  • Nutritional Support Kits
  • Home-based Care
  • Support Group Meetings



Skill Development

We empower women and youth with vocational skills to help them become financially independent. Our tailoring and computer centers provide free or subsidized training.

  • Tailoring Institute
  • Computer Training Center
  • Livelihood Assistance

Our Statement of Beliefs

  • We believe in one loving God revealed in the Bible, who created and continues to sustain the universe. God exists eternally in three persons: the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit.

  • We believe the Bible is the inspired and written Word of Godâ€”accurate, trustworthy, and fully authoritative in all that it teaches. Through Scripture, God reveals the meaning of life and the way to true and living faith.

  • We believe God created mankind in His own image for perfect fellowship with Him. Through disobedience, this relationship was broken, creating a spiritual emptiness in the human heart. This relationship is restored only through the sacrificial work of Jesus Christ.

  • We believe the Church is the community of all who believe in Jesus Christ. It is the living presence of Christ in the world, called to proclaim the Good News and to transform lives through love, service, and faithful witness.

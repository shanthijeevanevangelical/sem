Where We Serve

Our mission stations are strategically located in rural and tribal belts where the gospel has not yet been fully proclaimed.

Kottaibyrahalli

Morappur

Every Sunday, we hold a worship service. On the first Sunday of each month, the Dharmapuri PLHA gathering is conducted. We distribute tracts, Bibles, and Christian education books, and also organize special gatherings such as youth and childrenâ€™s meetings. Bible College sessions are conducted every 2nd Saturday and 3rd Thursday. We provide Vacation Bible School (VBS) training through IBL & CEF and distribute free VBS materials to local pastors. In addition, computer and tailoring classes are conducted regularly. Twice a year, we organize free medical camps for the community.

Poovalmaduvu

Thoppur

Every Sunday, we conduct a worship service followed by Sunday class. After the service, we have a love feast, and we distribute tracts and Bibles. On every 3rd Sunday, special activities are organized. On weekdays from 2 PM to 4 PM, tailoring and computer classes are held. Every year, we give Christmas gifts to children and share the Christmas gospel with them. We also conduct Vacation Bible School (VBS) every May.

Sellamudi

Mettur Dam Back Side

We conduct worship services every Sunday, followed by Sunday classes. We distribute tracts and Bibles to everyone and run adult literacy programs. On weekdays, tailoring classes are held from 10 AM to 4 PM in three nearby villages. Every year, we give Christmas gifts to children and church members and share the Christmas gospel with them. We also conduct Vacation Bible School (VBS) every May to teach and inspire local children.
